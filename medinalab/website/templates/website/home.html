Mission

Our research group is committed to advancing precision oncology through cutting-edge immunotherapy. By integrating clinical insights with experimental research, we aim to identify biomarkers that predict treatment response and to uncover novel targets for therapeutic intervention. We investigate a broad range of immunotherapeutic approaches, including immune checkpoint blockade and adoptive cell therapies such as CAR-T and tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes (TILs).
